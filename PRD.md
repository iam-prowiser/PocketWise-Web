# PocketWise – Smart Money Management for Students

## Problem Statement

College students frequently face challenges in managing their personal finances. Common problems include overspending, poor budgeting habits, irregular expense tracking, difficulty saving money, and limited financial literacy.

PocketWise provides students with an intelligent platform for expense tracking, budgeting, savings guidance, and financial education.

---

# Goals

## Primary Goal

Help students manage their money effectively through expense tracking, budgeting, savings guidance, and financial education.

## Secondary Goals

* Reduce overspending.
* Encourage regular expense tracking.
* Improve budgeting habits.
* Promote financial awareness.
* Help students achieve savings goals.
